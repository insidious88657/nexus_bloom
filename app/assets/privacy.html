Last Updated: February 19, 2026

TL;DR: All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or sell anything. Federation is 100% anonymous model deltas only.

1. Data Collection

NexusBloom is a privacy-first AI companion. We collect:

  • Zero personal information
  • Zero voice recordings (processed locally, never transmitted)
  • Zero user identifiers

2. Data Storage

All data is stored exclusively on your device using SQLite:

  • Voice journal entries (text only, no audio)
  • AI-generated insights
  • Energy levels and contribution counters
  • Profile name (if you choose to set one)

This data never leaves your device except as anonymous model deltas (see Federated Learning).

3. Microphone Permission

We request microphone access for:

  • Voice journaling: Convert your speech to text using on-device speech recognition
  • Audio is processed locally and immediately discarded
  • Only text transcriptions are saved (on your device only)

4. On-Device AI

NexusBloom uses TensorFlow Lite for 100% local inference:

  • All AI processing happens on your device
  • No cloud API calls
  • No data sent to third-party services
  • Your insights are yours alone

5. Federated Learning (Optional)

If you choose to contribute to humanity's collective Bloom:

  • We upload anonymous model deltas only (mathematical gradients, no personal data)
  • Deltas are aggregated using the FedAvg algorithm
  • Your contributions are completely anonymous
  • No voice recordings, text, or identifiable information is ever transmitted
  • You can opt out at any time

6. Third-Party Services

NexusBloom does not use:

  • Analytics platforms (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Crash reporting services (no Sentry, Crashlytics)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:

  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • No cloud backups or remote copies exist
  • Your data is truly gone

8. Children's Privacy

NexusBloom does not knowingly collect data from anyone. Since we collect zero personal information, users of all ages can use the app safely.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of any material changes via app update notes. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about privacy? Open an issue on our GitHub repo: github.com/insidious88657/nexus_bloom

Our Promise: We built NexusBloom because we believe AI should enhance humanity, not surveil it. Your consciousness is sacred. Your data is yours.
